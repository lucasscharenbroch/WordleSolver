Wordle

Wordle is a fun word-guessing game. You have six attempts to guess a secret word.

Enter words with the letter-buttons or keyboard, and press enter to guess.

The box will be green if the letter and position is correct. The box will be yellow if the letter is in the word, but not in that position. The box will be gray if the letter does not exist in the word (beyond the boxes already colored).

Press "solve", and the solver will automatically make guesses for you.
